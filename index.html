A surprising number of SMBs are still running on aging phone systems — PBXs, PRIs, carrier-locked boxes, or hardware that hasn’t been touched in a decade.

When these systems fail, the entire business goes dark. No inbound calls, no routing, no mobile fallback, no cloud redundancy. It’s a single point of failure that most teams don’t notice until it breaks.

Modern cloud telephony (Teams Phone, Nextiva, S-Net, 8x8, Five9, SIP platforms, etc.) solves these issues, but many organizations aren’t sure where to start.
